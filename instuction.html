PRACTICE TEST

Disclaimer

1) This is a demo for a "Computer Based Examination" for your practice and familiarization purpose only.

2) The actual question paper for the examination consist of 200 questions to be attempted in 2 hours time and this "Practice test" consist of 25 questions to be attempted in 15 minutes.

About Question Paper

1) The Question Paper consists of multiple choice objective type questions with 4 options out of which only 1 is correct.

2) The computer based exam will be bilingual i.e. English & Hindi languages.

3) There is a TIMER (Clock) available on the top right corner of the screen.

4) Each question carry 1 mark.

5) Every correct answer will get 1 mark and there will be negative marking where 0.25 marks will be deducted for each wrong answer.

6) Only one question will be displayed on the computer screen at a time.

7) The questions can be answered in any order within the given time frame.

8) Candidates can change the answer later if required.

9) To move between questions candidates should use Next Question or Previous Question buttons.

10) Answers will be saved automatically when you move to another question.

11) Candidates have the option to bookmark a question for review.

12) Question palette on the right side of the screen shows question status.

13) On completion of exam duration the test will automatically submit.

14) Candidate will be able to submit the test only after completing the exam time.
